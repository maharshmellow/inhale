Privacy Policy

We don't collect any information.

Inhale was created to help decrease stress, increase focus, and improve your overall life. This doesn't require harvesting user data.
  • We don't store your name, email, id, or any other personally identifiable information
  • Your custom breathing patterns are stored only on your device
  • No data is transferred from your device to us (we don't even have a server)
  • No analytics data is collected on the app
  • No data is shared with advertisers (we don't have ads)
For more information, contact us at inhale@maharsh.net
